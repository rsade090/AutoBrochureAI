# Hugging Face: The AI Community Building the Future

## Who We Are
Hugging Face is a collaborative platform at the forefront of artificial intelligence and machine learning. 
Our mission is to empower the AI community by providing tools, models, datasets, and applications that foster innovation and creativity. 
We believe that collaboration fuels progress, and our platform is designed to support Machine Learning (ML) enthusiasts, developers, 
researchers, and enterprises alike.

## Our Offerings
- **Models:** Access over 1 million models contributed by the community, including state-of-the-art solutions for various AI tasks 
  from natural language processing to image generation.
- **Datasets:** Explore a rich library of over 250,000 datasets to support your projects and experiments.
- **Spaces:** Experience live applications powered by AI technologies in a variety of domains.
- **Enterprise Solutions:** Tailored packages for businesses, including optimized compute resources and enterprise-grade support. 
  Starting at $20/user/month.
